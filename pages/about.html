About

This site is designed to help claimants understand their rights when the DWP asks for personal and financial information such as bank statements. It explains key ideas like legal basis, necessity, and proportionality in plain language, and offers template wording you can adapt for your own letters. Nothing here is formal legal advice, but it is intended to give you clearer language, more confidence, and a starting point for asking fair questions about how your data is used.
